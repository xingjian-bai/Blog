Self-Portraits

Artwork by FrānkǎnLīsà and Elliott Wu



Pareidolic faces are images in the natural world on which we subconsciously cast a humanoid interpretation. These are moments we find ourselves in an unexpected encounter with another being. They are mirrors through which we see the images of ourselves as humans.

This artwork visualizes pareidolic faces in 3D using an unsupervised machine learning model trained on human face images.
