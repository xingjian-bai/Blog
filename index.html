Xingjian Bai


I am an undergraduate student at Oxford, interested in algorithms and machine learning. My research has focused on online algorithms, learning-augmented algorithms, and theory-inspired machine learning model design. I am starting a PhD at MIT EECS Department.

During my time at Oxford, I have had the privilege of working with Jan Obloj, Christian Coester, Christian Rupprecht, and Luke Melas-Kyriazi. Additionally, I have spent an enriching summer at Stanford, working with Jiajun Wu, Weiyu Liu, and Joy Hsu.


Collaborators: I am always excited to learn from and collaborate with people from all places. If you want to discuss ideas with me, feel free to reach out! You can either email me or book my chatting hours .
